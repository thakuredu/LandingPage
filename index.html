About TCET
Deemed to be University

Thakur College of Engineering & Technology (TCET) is a deemed to be University established in AY 2001 02, committed to providing quality technical education aligned with international standards and global requirements. The institute is ISO 9001 2015, ISO 14001 2015, and ISO 50001 2018 certified, NAAC accredited with an A grade, and offers multiple NBA accredited programmes. TCET has been recognised with the Asia Pacific Quality Award for Best in Class and as an Excellent Performing and Innovative Engineering Institute in Maharashtra. With strong academics, state of the art infrastructure, and a focus on innovation and research, TCET develops engineers and leaders ready for the modern world.

Our Vision

TCET will excel in Technical, Professional and Research Education to become an internationally renowned multidisciplinary premier institute of engineering, technology, computer application, management studies and allied professional education.

Our Mission

To provide state-of-the-art infrastructure and right academic ambience for UG, PG and Ph.D. programmes. To nurture technical competence and research aptitude amongst aspiring graduates by imparting agile, holistic and multidisciplinary education. To enhance career opportunities for students by collaborating with reputed industry, academic and research institutes. To make students ethical and responsible citizens, both locally and globally competent.

Why TCET

Life at TCET University embodies holistic excellence, fostering academic, professional, and personal growth in every student. With a 25-year legacy, TCET is a deemed to be university recognised for delivering world-class education aligned with global standards. The universitys diverse and inclusive community welcomes students from across India and abroad, creating a vibrant multicultural learning environment. Innovation thrives through state-of-the-art laboratories and advanced research centres, while student life is enriched with dynamic clubs, experiential workshops, and international exposure programmes that inspire leadership, creativity, and global competence within a supportive and engaging academic ecosystem.
